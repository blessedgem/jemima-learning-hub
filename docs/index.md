# Jemima Learning Hub

Welcome to my lifelong learning hub.

This space organizes my notes, reflections, research ideas, technical learning, and professional development across cybersecurity, blockchain, AI governance, privacy-preserving systems, research writing, teaching, leadership, and career growth.

## Purpose

The purpose of this hub is to help me:

- Organize concepts clearly
- Build long-term knowledge
- Connect learning to research and practice
- Track ideas for papers, proposals, projects, and presentations
- Develop a searchable personal knowledge base

## How to Use This Hub

Use one page per concept. Each concept page should explain the idea simply, connect it to practice, and record references or examples.

Recommended concept note structure:

1. Simple definition
2. Why it matters
3. Key terms
4. How it works
5. Practical example
6. Research or career connection
7. References
